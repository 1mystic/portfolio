Time

Feb 8, 2026 • 2 min read • By Atharv Khare

A meditation on time's fleeting nature and the importance of cherishing each moment.

Time, a fleeting force that can't be held,
It passes by slowly, but never does it ever dwell.
It's the essence of life, it's what binds us together,
No matter how hard we try, it will never last forever.

It can bring us joy and it can bring us pain,
it can take us back to the old, or bring us to the new again.
It can make us laugh and it can make us cry,
It's the one thing that we can never deny.

So we must cherish it, use it well,
For time is a gift, of which we can never tell.
It can be hard to understand,
but we must try,
For time is precious and it will someday fly.
