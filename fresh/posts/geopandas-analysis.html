Geospatial Analysis with GeoPandas

Nov 10, 2025 • 6 min read • By Atharv Khare

Numbers tell you what happened. Geospatial data tells you where it happened. And in the world of logistics and food delivery, 'where' is often more important than 'what'.

In this post, I explore a real-world dataset of 5,000 food delivery orders in Bhopal, using GeoPandas and Folium to uncover spatial trends that raw spreadsheets obscure.

Choropleth Mapping with Folium

Visualising order density across different localities reveals stark economic and demographic divides. A spatial join between the order coordinates and municipal boundary polygons allows us to aggregate demand efficiently.
